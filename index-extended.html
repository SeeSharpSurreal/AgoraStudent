Welcome to AgoraStudent

You are never too young to be an entrepreneur. This website is a place for the students of a middle school business planning class in Los Angeles to sell their products online. To learn more, visit our About page.

Marketing

Students will have valuable experiences with selling their own products and then shipping them.
