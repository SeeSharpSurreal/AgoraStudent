Welcome to AgoraStudent

AgoraStudent is a place where students learning about business at their schools can sell their products and expand to a global market. This website is based at a small middle school in Los Angeles. The school has a business planning class where students learn about how to start their very own businesses and sell products of their own. However, their market is limited to the school and the little places they can visit (e.g. Trader Joe's). We developed this website so that they can sell their products online and sell to anyone out there. Eventually, other schools that teach business will be able to join us and use our site to sell their products too.

Buying from AgoraStudent means you are supporting young entrepreneurs.

Why are we doing this?

We're doing this because we want to prove to the world that nobody is too young to start a business. It doesn't matter how old you are. Ever seen kids with lemonade stands? That's a business.

But we wanted to go beyond the lemonade (don't get us wrong, we love lemonade). We wanted to bring an opportunity for students to start selling anything they want, whenever they want, wherever they want. And AgoraStudent was born.

We're going to bring business to children's lives. They will learn the ins and outs of business, and once they're ready, they'll be able to sell whatever they want on this site and make a difference in the world.
