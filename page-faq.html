Students who run businesses in school and wish to sell online will send in an application form and will be able to place up to five products on the website at a time. Once they have an order, they will be in charge of shipping the order and making sure that they satisfy their customers.

Don't worry, the food products are safe to eat. They create their food carefully, in ways such as keeping their hands sterile and not letting the food sit around for too long. They will find the packaging that is correct for their purposes and even in the unlikely event that something does go wrong, you will be refunded.

It depends on the item and the group. Different groups may have different return policies for their items, and food products are not returnable at all.
